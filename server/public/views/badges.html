Empathy Achievement Badges are self-awarded. Don’t worry, we trust you! To find out how to reach these key milestones in your kindness practice, click on the badges. Can your family collect them all?

Generosity Badge

Awarded for making a donation to a cause you love. Visit our Donate: Charitable Giving & Fun Fundraisers collection for creative ideas to make generosity memorable.

Bibliophile Badge

Awarded for reading a kindness-themed book and discussing it. Visit our Read Together collection for book ideas and discussion questions.

Smile Sharing Badge

Awarded for reaching out to others who may feel lonely and isolated. Visit our Provide Comfort collection for ideas.

Big-Hearted Royalty Badge

Awarded for making a donation to Doing Good Together™ to help new families around the country start a habit of giving. Visit our Give section to make your contribution.

Creature Care Badge

Awarded for completing an act of kindness for the animal kingdom. Visit our Care for Animals collection for ideas.

Citizenship Badge

Awarded for engaging in a social justice issue your family cares about. Visit our Advocacy: Peace, Justice, and Social Action collection for ideas.

DGT™ Super Fan Badge

Awarded for signing up for any of our free subscription services. Check out our monthly newsletter, weekly blog, and monthly local volunteer listings.

Happy Habits Badge

Awarded for fitting a bite-sized, regular act of kindness into your family’s weekly routine. Visit our Practice Kindness collection for ideas!

Helpful Holiday Badge

Awarded for adding intentional kindness to your family holiday traditions. Visit our Adopt Holiday Traditions collection for ideas.

Flash Kindness Badge

Awarded for completing a quick, possibly random, act of kindness. Visit our Share Quick Acts of Kindness collection for ideas.

Wilderness Hero Badge

Awarded for protecting the environment. Visit our Heal the Earth collection for ideas!

Urgent Needs Badge

Awarded for completing a project that helps those who struggle to earn enough money for basic needs, like housing, food, and electricity. Visit our Fight Poverty collection for ideas.

Big-Hearted Badges are automatically awarded when you hit important milestones in your kindness practice. Do your best to collect them all!

On Your Way Badge

Awarded for completing and recording your first project! Keep going, and watch your progress on the Do Good Dozen Challenge board.

Halfway Badge

Awarded for reaching the halfway mark in your Do Good Dozen Challenge. Way to go! Complete six more projects to reach your goal.

Champion Badge

Awarded for completing and recording 12 projects in less than 12 months. You’ve made a difference, made memories, and started a new habit of kindness. We’ll contact you about your prize shortly!

Do Good Downloads Badge

Kindness Ambassador Badge

Awarded for sharing one of your kindness projects on social media or at Doing Good Together. Your stories of kindness will inspire new families to get involved.

Kindness Coach Badge

Awarded for encouraging another big-hearted family by liking a project they’ve shared. Your support keeps others motivated.

Bright Smiles Badge

Awarded for sharing a photo of your family’s kindness in action.
